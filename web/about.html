About Me

As a female artist and designer, Danielle's work tends to focus on the beauty of femininity that is so often lost or looked down upon in our society. She strives to help women feel confident in their bodies through fashion, rather than feed into insecurities for profit. She loves to tie themes of sustainability with the confidence that radiates when embracing femininity through fashion design.
